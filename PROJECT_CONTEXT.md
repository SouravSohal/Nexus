# PROJECT CONTEXT

## Project Name

**NEXUS – The Cognitive Supply Chain Twin**

**Tagline**
From Breaking News to Business Impact in Seconds.

---

# Vision

NEXUS is an AI-powered Decision Intelligence Platform that transforms unstructured real-world events into actionable business decisions.

Rather than acting as another chatbot, NEXUS continuously analyzes disruption events, propagates their impact through a digital supply-chain graph, and recommends optimal mitigation strategies.

The first implementation focuses on supply-chain resilience, but the architecture is intentionally designed so that it can later support cybersecurity, healthcare, finance, disaster response, and infrastructure monitoring.

---

# Problem Statement

Modern enterprises rely on thousands of suppliers distributed across the world.

When an unexpected disruption occurs—

* Port strike
* Factory shutdown
* Political conflict
* Natural disaster
* Supplier bankruptcy

—companies spend hours or even days determining:

* Which suppliers are affected?
* Which products are impacted?
* Which factories will stop production?
* What should be done immediately?

The delay costs millions.

NEXUS reduces this analysis from hours to seconds.

# Demo Scenario

Initial State

Everything is healthy.

Graph is green.

KPIs show no disruptions.

User pastes a news article:

"Port of Antwerp workers begin a 5-day strike."

System automatically:

• extracts entities
• identifies affected logistics routes
• propagates impact through the supply graph
• estimates affected products
• estimates financial impact
• recommends alternative suppliers

Graph animates from green to red.

Dashboard updates instantly.
